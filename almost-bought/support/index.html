Common questions

How do I add an item?
Share a product link from your browser or shopping app into Almost Bought using the share sheet. It'll parse the price, name, and image and add it to your hold list.

Where is my data stored?
Everything you save lives locally on your device — there's no account and no server. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?
Open Settings inside the app and choose "Delete all local data."

An item didn't parse correctly (wrong price, name, or image).
Email us the store name and, if possible, the product link so we can improve support for that site.
